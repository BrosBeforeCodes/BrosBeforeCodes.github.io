Mission

Ballers without violence exists to bring together people together to talk and play basketball.

About Us

Ballers without violence is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a safe space for people to meet and discuss their histories of overcoming violence.

we organize pick up basketball games across the GTA to reach people in their communities to create places free of violence.
